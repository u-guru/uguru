    • Pull to refresh, more info
    • Craigslist redesign
    • onEnter and onExit rotateOut
    • Collections
    • Product purchase
    • Circle expand
    • Card onHover animation, i.e. household profile tile

    Notes

    • Differences should be represented by visuals; similarities/unification should be represented by animation
    • Mobile animations should have more "internal" animations due to lack of screen real estate
    • Most mobile animations tend to be conservative, valuing smoothness over complexity
    • "Skeumorphic" animation is more important for mobile because touching your screen is more tactile than being separated from your screen by a mouse on desktop
