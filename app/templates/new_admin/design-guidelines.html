Each Individual Workflow, Screen, or Component must follow these rules:

  • As simple as possible. Each component must serve a purpose
  • Design should look great independent of any dependencies that involve animations
  • Padding should follow an example on Jon's flintos'.
  • Colors should feel like they are in place. Nothing appears dull at first glance. Contrasts should be immediately apparent.
  • For anything that we dont have an example from our guide / Jon's flintos, you must find a popular, well-designed app to start as a foundation. Then apply the previous steps above this bullet point.
