Rules / 0 Tolerance Policy

  • Being late is unacceptable, not giving a heads-up is even worse.
  • No Surprises. Anything < 2 weeks are unacceptable, if possibly controllable.
  • No attitude , egos, or talking back - unless you are 100% confident in what you are saying / doing. Please don't embarass yourself.
  • For any task with particular instructions, follow them carefully & precisely. If you are confused - tell me! Dont let 4 hours pass by and then we're both frustrated.
  • No passive agression - say what you feel privately to me if something is annoying you. I do not expect anyone to keep something to themselves & let it build up.
  • Do not lie, cheat, or bullshit me. Even worse, dont let me find out on my own
  • Follow directions accordingly & precisely. If you think you have a better solution, propose it to me first before you make the decision yourself.
  • Do not bring external problems into the workplace. Why be selfish ruin & someone elses energy?
  • If there is some personal issue or question - DO NOT ask it in front of everyone. Ask me privately.
  • You are NOT entitled to choose whether you can work remotely. You are expected to be at the office every single day, 6 days a week, unless you have a really really good reason.
  • If fuck something up really bad , whatever it is, don't just tell me. (even worse, dont let me figure out on my own). Come forth with a solution of how you are going to clean up that mess.

Expectations

  1. Provide significant value. Your goal should be to do things faster than I can, so you become inherently useful on a daily basis. This takes time, and no one has achieved this yet (in the current company as of June 26th).
  2. Follow best practices. Learn from the experts online or relative to you (maybe me). Do not be stubborn and do what you think is right (or at least propose first). That only takes steps backward.
  3. Your mentality should be to be the best in your field
  4. 100% energy levels MUST BE existent when you come in to work. Negative energy is extremely contagious.
  5. Respect for everyone's peace & productivity in the workplace
  6. During the times you are working on Uguru, put the company before yourself . Basically, follow best practices versus what you think.
  7. Complete transparency - do not hesitate to tell me what is bothering you or what is going on. Consequences will always be worse if I find out on my own.
  8. Breaks can be useful , but need to be justifiable. Anything over 15 minutes requires approval.
  9. Clean up after yourselves

Surprise / Make-up Policy

If you missed work for whatever reason & it was unexpectation or < 2 weeks notice.

Whatever the situation, whether it was health, chance, random, etc event, here's what you can to do make up:

Option One
  1. Count the number of hours you missed & let Samir know. Each day missed = 8 hours to make up.
  2. Propose the makeup time periods over the course of the next two weeks (anything after two weeks is invalid and will not be contacted towards your pay).
  3. Create a spreadsheet with your planned schedule & have me approve it. The five columns should be time, date, total hours, description of task, approved by samir.
  4. If approval is received, continually update this spreadsheet until all your hours are made up.

Option Two
  1. You are free to opt out and say 'Samir dont pay me for these days on the next 1st of the month. Just subtract the daily amount'

Daily Schedule

  • Morning 8am meeting. You must be 100% energetic & ready for the day. You have either brought breakfast or have already consumed it.
  • 11:45am lunch meeting in the courtyard.
  • 4pm checkout time @ courtyard
  • Every hour on the hour, we are going to do standup.

    Each person is required to answer
    1. What did you do the past hour?
    2. Did you finish what you were expecting to complete?
    3. How can you do better the next hour, if there was something you could have improved on?
  • We are going to do less of Samir micro-managing. You will receive a set of tasks (small, large, any size)

    Each person is required to maintain themselves & propose the following
    1. How long will it take for you to complete this task?
    2. Why? Is there a more effecient / effective way? (dont let me come up with a more efficient way!)
    3. Do you have questions about this task?
      (you are required to ask up-front, or immediately once you have that question, in other words,--> no surprises! I don't want to hear "you were busy so i just went ahead and did ______" <-- WRONG)
  • If you are stuck more than 15 minutes, you are required to let me immediately know. If I am busy, you are also required to move onto another task until I am free to help you.

Pay, Housing Policy

  • Payroll will be processed on the 1st of each month (from here on forward, July 1st), for the previous month. You will most likely receive either the check or wire before the 5th.
  • You will be rewarded with options or higher pay on a monthly basis if you exceed expectations. This will be quite clear when you do so.
  • I do not cover your transportation unless you are in validation mode, or you are a special case.
  • If you qualify for housing stipend, you will receive $800 monthly --> that is your budget. You have the freedom to spend outside of that.
