Surprise / Make-up Policy

If you missed work for whatever reason & it was unexpectation or < 2 weeks notice.

Whatever the situation, whether it was health, chance, random, etc event, here's what you can to do make up:

Option One

  1. Count the number of hours you missed & let Samir know. Each day missed = 8 hours to make up.
  2. Propose the makeup time periods over the course of the next two weeks (anything after two weeks is invalid and will not be contacted towards your pay).
  3. Create a spreadsheet with your planned schedule & have me approve it. The five columns should be time, date, total hours, description of task, approved by samir.
  4. If approval is received, continually update this spreadsheet until all your hours are made up.

Option Two

  1. You are free to opt out and say 'Samir dont pay me for these days on the next 1st of the month. Just subtract the daily amount'

Option Three

  1. Ask me to give you something challenging for make-up
