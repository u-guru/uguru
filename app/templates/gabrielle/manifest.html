Manifesto

For students by students.

An entire platform devoted meet the on-demand needs of college students. Why struggle when there is someone footsteps away who’s mastered the exact skillset you need to learn?

We understand the needs of our students - in fact, our entire team is composed of part-time students and recent graduates! Learn more about us here.

No student left behind.

Private tutoring is expensive and we aim to provide solutions that makes on-demand academic support available & affordable to all demographics.

By allowing barter and eliminating transaction fees entirely, we are extremely determined to find a way to grow without ridiculous pricing.

Your data is yours, and no one else's.

We will never sell your data to third-party platforms.

If a third party is interested in accessing your data (i.e. recruiters, survey platforms), we plan to ask your permission first, and share the love ($$) with you each time.

Find your inner guru.

Everyone is born with talents, and there is absolutely someone around you who could use it. Our platform inherently promotes knowledge exchange, skill-set barter, and much more.

Your money is no good here.

We are convinced that we can find a way to keeping our core services & transactions free. Any new changes to pricing, we’ll let you know first and keep things 100% transparent.

Join Us! Help make college even more awesome.

Become a guru, setup your profile, and help your fellow peers excel in school.
