Students are tutors, tutors are students

When you need help you're a student. When you help others you're a tutor. As simple as that. Peer to peer at its finest.

How we validate tutors

Every tutor begins at a fresh slate at uGuru.me for every skill they've added. If the quality is good, the rate for that tutor will increase. Any tutor below a certain average (around 3 stars) will be much less promoted.

How our pricing works

Each tutor begins at a certain rate. With more tutoring encounters through uGuru.me and high ratings, we'll market you at a higher rate. Base price is $7/hour.

We may charge a transaction fee in the future. Right now everything is free, no credit card required!
